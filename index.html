Our Story

Ten years ago, we opened a burger stand in a repurposed auto shop in Georgetown. Born from the dreams of a trucker and his son, Seattle Burgers Inc. grew from a flickering neon sign to a local staple known for bold flavors and a no-nonsense attitude. We've weathered storms — literal and metaphorical — and came out stronger. Now, we're ready for our next chapter, and we want you to be in it.
